Rose

Rose is one of the most beautiful flowers among several others. It has beautiful petals and a stem that is usually full of thorns. Roses can be found in many colours, our of which red and white are more common. Apart from that, there are pink and yellow roses too.

Lotus

It is a beautiful flower which has an appealing fragrance and colour. The flower is usually white or light pink in colour with boat-shaped petals. The leaves of a lotus flower are large and dark green in colour. The stem of the flower is very thick and it has a glowing yellow centre.

mogra

The mogra plant is native to South and Southeast Asia, but it is now grown all over the world.

sunflower

Sunflowers are large flowers with a hairy stem that can grow up to 15 feet tall. Their leaves are broad and serrated, and are arranged in spirals on the stem.
